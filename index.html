About Me

Ketema Kebede

Entrepreneur

I started my career as a sales executive over 20 years ago, where I learned a lot about marketing and negotiation. Now, I’m a business developer, entrepreneur, and  marketing expert who loves helping businesses grow. Over the years, I’ve started companies in Media, IT, E-commerce, and Import & Export, working with both small and large businesses to create successful partnerships.

To give back to the community, I founded "Habesha Expat," a platform that supports the Ethiopian expat community and helps Ethiopian-owned businesses in Dubai and the Gulf region.

Right now, I’m leading Adamant Holding Company, which focuses on international trade, investment advice, and property management. We also manage other businesses in import/export, engineering, and investment, with operations in Addis Ababa, Dubai, and beyond. My team and I are working to create smart, sustainable solutions for the global market.

I’m here to help you with your business, whether you’re just getting started or ready to grow. My goal is to provide honest, real advice to help you achieve your dreams.
